Artist Statement

As a Digital Storyteller, I aim to create content that executes and tells stories through a creative lens. Whether that be music, health, fashion, culture, gender, comedy, etc., I incorporate several areas and genres into my work. My projects and publications include short films, graphic design, photography, commercial advertisement, and content writing. My work exemplifies my experience which gives me the tools to continue working with a wide range of interdisciplinary approaches to tell stories through a digital lens.
