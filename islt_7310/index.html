Mary Galkowski is a Digital Storytelling student at the University of Missouri- Columbia, expected to graduate in May of 2020. The Digital Storytelling program she is emphasizing in is production. The production emphasis consists of creating documentary and short films that tell a story for an audience in which educates and maximizes visibility with proven results. The Digital Storytelling program’s goal is to educate students with experience in Adobe Creative Suite as well as assemble a clear and concise portfolio website, and generate career and professional documents during the Capstone course. Mary’s areas of interest include social media, photography, film, public relations, writing, and graphic design.
